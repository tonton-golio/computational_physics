# Applied Statistics

Every measurement you'll ever make is contaminated by randomness. Your instruments aren't perfect, your samples are finite, and the universe just won't sit still. The tools in this topic -- estimation, testing, fitting, model selection -- exist for one reason: to tell you how much you actually know, and how much you're guessing.

If you ever have to decide whether a bridge will fall down or a drug actually works, this is the toolkit that keeps you honest.
